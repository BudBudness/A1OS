Education OS — Fees Module
===========================

PURPOSE:
Manage school fee structures, student obligations, balances, invoices, and financial status.

FEE LIFECYCLE:
  fee structure defined
      ↓
  student obligation generated
      ↓
  invoice / charge issued
      ↓
  payment received
      ↓
  receipt recorded
      ↓
  balance recalculated
      ↓
  account status updated

CORE RECORDS:
  - academic period
  - class / level
  - fee type
  - amount
  - student
  - invoice reference
  - amount paid
  - balance
  - due date
  - financial status
  - audit history
