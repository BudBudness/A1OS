Education OS — Students Module
===============================

PURPOSE:
Manage the complete student lifecycle from application to active enrollment.

LIFECYCLE:
  applicant
      ↓
  admitted
      ↓
  enrolled
      ↓
  active student
      ↓
  graduated / withdrawn / archived

CORE RECORD:
  - student identity
  - guardian information
  - contact information
  - date of birth
  - class / level
  - enrollment status
  - admission reference
  - audit history
