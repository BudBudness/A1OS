Education OS — Payments Module
===============================

PURPOSE:
Record and reconcile payments against student financial obligations.

PAYMENT WORKFLOW:
  payment initiated
      ↓
  payment received
      ↓
  payment verified
      ↓
  payment allocated
      ↓
  receipt generated
      ↓
  student balance updated

PAYMENT METHODS:
  - cash
  - mobile money
  - bank transfer
  - card
  - other approved method

CORE RECORDS:
  - payment reference
  - student
  - invoice / obligation
  - amount
  - payment method
  - transaction reference
  - verification status
  - receipt reference
  - timestamp
  - audit history
